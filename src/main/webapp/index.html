About Appu Chat's

Welcome to Appu Chat's Center — Kalaburagi's most loved street food destination, nestled near ECI Sedam Road.

For over a decade, we've been serving fresh, spicy, and authentic Indian chaat made with hand-picked ingredients and the finest spices, bringing the true flavours of India's streets right to your plate.

Every bite tells a story of passion, tradition, and taste. Come hungry, leave happy!
